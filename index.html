Hi, I'm Grace!

Hey there! My name is Grace Garmo, and I'm a sophomore at the University of Michigan studying User Experience (UX) Design in the U-M School of Information with minors in Philosophy and Science, Technology, and Society. I'm an incoming Product Desgn Intern at Intuit where I'll be working on the user interface of their mobile apps this summer! On campus, I'm involved in Kappa Theta Pi Professional Technology Fraternity and VOID Tech Consulting. Through those two student organizations, I work to improve upon my design skills and work on awesome products like creating mobile apps and websites! When I'm not in class, I love trying out new foods from around the world -- thus, I decided to make this page and show off my favorite food and drinks!


Food has always been my love language, whether it be bonding over a delicious meal or trying (and failing) to cook one of my favorite dishes for someone. On this site, I'll be showcasing my favorite foods and drinks from three regions: the Middle East, East and South Asia, and Central America. Hopefully, you'll discover a new favorite item and try it out for yourself!
